 This Project captures the Business layer of the three tiered architecture.

 The folders contain the following types of files:
 -------------------------------------------------

 * Interfaces	: contains interfaces internal to the Business layer classes
 * Services		: classes that provide Business layer logic/functions
 * Helpers		: classes that act as supporting/helping classes that do not
				  provide business layer logic
